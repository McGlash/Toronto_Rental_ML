About

This Application aims to provide users with a one stop solution for all of their rental information needs. There are over 100,000 new people moving into Toronto every year. Most of them move for work and are new immigrants. Our application not only provides the latest available rentals but also the safety of the neighbourhood and its accessibility to services such as schools and hospitals. This information can be critical for anyone who needs to make a quick decisions. We have also incorporated Machine Learning to Analyze the latest rental trends and give you the most up to date fair market rental price.
