About

This Application aims to provide users with a one stop solution for all of their rental information needs. There are over somenumber of new people moving into Toronto every year. Most of them move for work and are new immigrants. Our application not only provides the latest available rentals but also the safety of the neighborhood and its accessibility to services such as schools and hospitals. This information can be critical for anyone who needs to make a quick decisions. We have also incorporated Machine Learning to Analyze the latest rental trends and give you the most up to date fair market rental price.

Mission

Rental Information updated every day from Crageslist

Rental Trends Analysis and up to date fair market rental price

Availability of Community Services as compared to Crime rate in the area.

Vision

Higher acuracy for prediction of fair market rental price

Recommendation based on previously liked rentals

Rental Recommendation based on user's description
